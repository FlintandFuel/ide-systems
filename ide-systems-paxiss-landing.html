Paxiss product overview

Paxiss is a complete core banking system that runs as SaaS or on-premise. It handles member records, account types, savings and lending, share management, GL posting and all the reporting your auditors and regulators will ask for, while keeping everyday tasks simple for staff.

UM

User and member management

Register members, manage KYC and FICA checks, role-based access, member hierarchies and easy member search for staff who prefer practical tools over technical setups.

CF

Client functions and accounts

Current, savings, fixed deposits, and share accounts with flexible interest rules and clear transaction histories for members and auditors.

GL

Financial and GL management

Automated postings, cash denomination tracking, share management and configurable income definitions so your finance team reconciles faster.

CP

Compliance and forensic controls

Built-in FICA flows, CODI-ready exports and full audit trails designed for Prudential Authority reporting and internal control checks.

Frequently asked questions

Short, clear answers to the questions cooperative managers ask when choosing a core banking partner.

Is Paxiss compliant with the Cooperative Banks Act and Prudential Authority requirements?

Yes. Paxiss includes reporting workflows and CODI and FICA processes to support regulatory reporting and audits.

Can Paxiss be deployed in-house or do we need to use a cloud version?

Both options are available. Many CFIs choose our SaaS service for quick onboarding, while institutions with strict data control policies select on-premise installations.

How long does onboarding take and what support is included?

Onboarding depends on institution size and customisation. Typical SaaS onboarding is weeks, not months, and includes training, data migration assistance and a local support contact.

Is the system accessible to non-technical staff and available in Afrikaans?

Yes. The interface is designed for non-technical users and language options include English and Afrikaans to match staff preferences.
